Grizzly Bear

Except for females with new cubs, grizzlies are normally solitary.

  • Scientific Name:: Ursus arctos horribilis
  • Average Weight: 300-500 pounds
  • Average Lifespan: 22-26 years
  • Diet: Plants and animals
